# Instagrid

Instagrid is a simple iOS app that allows users to create photo collages in a fun and creative way. With Instagrid, you can choose from various layout options, add your favorite photos, and share your collages with friends and family.

## Features

- Choose from different collage layouts.
- Add photos from your device's photo library to the collage.
- Reorder and replace photos within the collage.
- Easy sharing options to social media platforms or save to your device

## Usage

- Launch the app and choose your preferred collage layout.
- Tap on each photo frame to select and add photos from your device's photo library.
- Drag and drop to reorder or replace photos within the collage.
- Once you're satisfied with your collage, use the share button to share it with others or save it to your device.

Happy collaging with Instagrid!
